    • What is Lost Ark?

    Its a free top down MMO ARPG set in a fantasy world. Similar to games like BDO (Black dessert online), FFXIV, and Maplestory. Its been out in Korea for 3 years and just got a release this year in other regions including the US. While this game has been released for 3 years the developers have decided to drip feed us content releasing new characters/raids at varied times with the hopes of getting us caught up to Korea by the end of 2023.
