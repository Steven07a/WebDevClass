    • We finished the story now what?

    Now that we've finished the story its time to play the endgame content. This includes Chaos dungeons, Guardian Raids, Abyssal dungeons, Una's Task and more. First ill go over the general power progression which is honing. Regualr materials for honing include silver, the corresponding shards and fragments as well as leapstones. Basically regular materials is the bare minumum needed to hone and increase the players power. Then why do we need additional material? Well this is because like many MMO's it gets much harder to increase power the higher you get. To give some context in the begining stages of honing for T1 and T2 the honing percentages range from 80-60% with each succsessful hone becoming harder and harder (This has changed due to a recent patch. The honing rates for the first 2 tiers is now 100% as the developers want you to reach T3 as thats where the bulk of content is.) but with the use of additional materials its possible to increase these honing chances by around 1-10% depending on the tier and level of the item you are honing. Overall additional materials is something you will use in the later stages of a tier as increasing a 90% to 100% chance to hit a hone does not feel as significant to boosting a 1% to 2%.
