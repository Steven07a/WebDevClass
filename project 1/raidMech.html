    • The endgame Raids

    Now that we know how to raise our Item level and boost our general power we can do the endgame content which is raids. Here ill be showing one mechanic of a multi part raid. Raids can range from 4-8 people instances where there is a singular boss similar to Guardian raids the main difference being that there are typically wipe mechanics. This raid specifically is a 8 man raid.

    120 HP bars

    Once Vykas hits 120 hp bars shes going to go into her first mechanic which is the color wing test. What will happen is vykas will become immune and untargetable. 3 Vykas silhouette will spawn at 2, 7, and 11 o clock. Each silhouette will raise a number of wings ranging from 0-2 and the people at the clock positions would need to call out the number of wings and there clock positions. Meanwhile the other 5 players will need to start picking up orbs arranged in a cross shape starting from the center of the map. After each player picks up there orb a buff will appear above there head either with a purple or red symbol. These players need to run to the center of the map and figure out which color is the majority as that determins which order is safe. If the majority is red then the order is 2 > 1 > 0 and if its purple then its the opposite 0 > 1 > 2. If you survived then you've passed the first mechanic.

    65 HP bars

    The second mechanic starts at 65 hp bars and again Vykas will become invincible and disapear then her black silhouettes will appear but this time they will be located around 12,5, and 7 o clock. 2 players should be assigned to each position with the remaining 2 players standing in the center reading the chat. The reason for this is because the silhouettes will pulse a total of 5 times with the color being either black or red. Atleast one of the players will need to keep track of the sequence of pulses typically by typing the sequence into chat. ex: 12 brbrb
    Afterwards the 2 players standing in the middle need to run to the sequences with 3 black orbs in them while only taking the last black orb in the sequence. if a different orb is taken that person will die and if a black orb does not get blocked then the whole party will die. Once this mechanic is complete then its mostly a damage check.
